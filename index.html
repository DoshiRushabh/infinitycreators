Quality Policy

It is the policy and overall business objective of INFINITY CREATORS to provide services of highest quality and in compliance with the clients specified requirements. Product Facility and Methodology

Our team of qualified engineers & technicians constitute our core manufacturing unit. Although, a vendor is selected after close scrutiny as per our standard assessment procedure, each incoming component is thoroughly tested as per quality manual, with the help of test equipments. A few testing jigs are developed in-house to facilitate efficient testing & remove non-conforming products at the incoming stage itself. After vigilant in-process quality control, the finished products like Inductive LED DRIVERS,SMPS,AVS(AUTO VOLTAGE SWITCH),OTHER ELECTRONICS PRODUCTS, etc. are again subjected to quality tests as required.
